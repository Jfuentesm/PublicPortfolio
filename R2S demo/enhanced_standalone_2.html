R2S Methodology Guide

A comprehensive approach to risk-based strategy

Methodology Overview

Introduction to Risk2Strategy

Risk2Strategy (R2S) is a comprehensive methodology that transforms traditional risk management into strategic opportunity. By systematically identifying, analyzing, and clustering risks, organizations can develop targeted capabilities and strategic initiatives that not only mitigate risks but create competitive advantage.

The R2S methodology consists of six integrated phases, each building upon the insights and deliverables of previous phases to create a holistic view of risk and opportunity.

Methodology Phases

Phase 1 Project Setup

Establish project foundations, scope, and stakeholder alignment to ensure successful execution.

  • Project Charter
  • Stakeholder Map
  • Project Timeline
  • Data Collection Plan

Phase 2 Risk Assessment

Systematically identify and assess organizational risks across all categories and business areas.

  • Risk Catalog
  • Risk Matrix
  • Risk Heat Map
  • Value Chain Risk Mapping

Phase 3 Impact Quantification

Quantify the potential financial and strategic impact of identified risks to prioritize focus areas.

  • Financial Impact Analysis
  • Risk Correlation Network
  • Value at Risk Assessment
  • Strategic Impact Scoring

Phase 4 Strategic Clustering

Group interrelated risks into strategic clusters to identify common root causes and opportunity areas.

  • Strategic Risk Clusters
  • Causal Network Analysis
  • Strategic Opportunity Map
  • Cluster Prioritization Framework

Phase 5 Capability Assessment

Assess current organizational capabilities against required capabilities to address strategic risk clusters.

  • Capability Radar
  • Capability Gap Analysis
  • Capability Development Prioritization
  • Capability Roadmap

Phase 6 Initiative Roadmap

Develop a comprehensive implementation roadmap with strategic initiatives to build required capabilities.

  • Implementation Waves
  • Initiative Portfolio
  • Resource Allocation Plan
  • Strategic Benefits Realization Plan

Key Principles

The R2S methodology is built on several key principles that guide its application:

  • Strategic Focus: Elevating risk management from a compliance exercise to a strategic enabler
  • Holistic Approach: Considering risks across the entire organization and value chain
  • Opportunity Mindset: Viewing risks as potential sources of competitive advantage
  • Quantitative Rigor: Using data-driven analysis to support decision-making
  • Stakeholder Alignment: Engaging key stakeholders throughout the process
  • Implementation Orientation: Focusing on actionable outcomes and measurable results

Application Context

The R2S methodology can be applied across various contexts and industries:

  • Corporate Strategy: Informing strategic planning cycles and initiatives
  • Digital Transformation: Identifying risks and opportunities in digital initiatives
  • Sustainability: Addressing ESG risks and developing sustainable business models
  • Operational Excellence: Improving operational resilience and efficiency
  • Innovation: De-risking innovation initiatives and finding new opportunity areas
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Assessing risks and strategic fit in M&A activities
