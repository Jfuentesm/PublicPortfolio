The Challenge Facing Medium Businesses

Limited Resources, Critical Risks

Medium-sized businesses face significant enterprise risks, but often lack the dedicated resources, expertise, or budget for the comprehensive Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) processes typically found in large corporations.

Identifying relevant risks, assessing their potential impact, and knowing how to mitigate them is a critical, yet often neglected, need for growing businesses.

Without proper risk management, medium businesses remain vulnerable to disruptions, compliance issues, and missed opportunities that could impact their growth and sustainability.

Our AI-Powered Solution

An accessible, AI-driven platform that guides medium businesses through a streamlined risk management process

Contextual Risk Identification

Our platform queries our proprietary risk database and uses AI to filter, adapt, and present the most relevant potential risks, tailored to your specific business profile.

AI-Assisted Assessment

Facilitates evaluation of potential impact and likelihood with AI guidance, automatically calculating risk scores and presenting a prioritized list of key risks requiring attention.

Practical Remediation Plans

Generates specific, practical, and actionable remediation plan recommendations for each high-priority risk, suitable to the context and resources of your medium business.

How AIRisk Works

A streamlined, intuitive workflow designed specifically for medium businesses

Profile Setup & Risk Identification

Provide basic company information (industry, size, geographic location, key operations). Our platform suggests relevant risks from our proprietary database, refined by AI according to your profile.

Review, confirm, and add risks as needed, ensuring a comprehensive assessment tailored to your specific business context.

Guided Assessment Process

Input data (or review AI suggestions) on the likelihood and impact of confirmed risks, using intuitive interfaces designed for non-specialists.

Actionable Dashboard & Remediation

Receive a prioritized risk dashboard and clear, actionable remediation steps for your top risks. No generic checklists, just specific actions your team can implement.

Export comprehensive reports for stakeholders, track remediation progress, and update your risk assessment as your business evolves.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does AIRisk compare to traditional risk consultants?

AIRisk offers a more affordable and efficient alternative to traditional consultants. While consultants typically require weeks of work and high fees, our platform provides immediate insights for a fraction of the cost. We combine AI technology with our proprietary risk database to deliver personalized recommendations comparable to human experts, but accessible 24/7 and continuously updated.

How secure is my company's data on the platform?

Security is our top priority. AIRisk employs bank-level encryption, strict access controls, and regular security audits. We comply with GDPR and other regional data protection regulations. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties, and you maintain full ownership of all information you provide to the platform.

Do I need risk management expertise to use the platform?

No specialized expertise is required. AIRisk is specifically designed for medium businesses without dedicated risk management teams. Our intuitive interface guides you through each step with clear explanations and AI assistance. The platform translates complex risk concepts into practical, actionable information that any business leader can understand and implement.

How long does it take to get started and see results?

Most customers complete their initial risk assessment within 1-2 hours after signing up. Our guided setup process quickly captures your business context, and our AI immediately begins generating relevant risk insights. You'll receive your first risk dashboard and remediation recommendations the same day, allowing you to start implementing improvements right away.
